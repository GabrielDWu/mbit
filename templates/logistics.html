Logistics

Time and Location

mBIT will be held at Montgomery Blair High School on Saturday, November 2, 2019 from 9:00 AM to 2:30 PM. Our address is 51 University Blvd E, Silver Spring, MD 20901.

Here is the schedule:

8:30 - 9:00 AMRegistration, team setup
9:00 - 9:15 AMOpening Statements
9:30 AMContest Begins
12:00 PMLunch
1:30 PMContest Closes
1:30 - 2:00 PMFun Round
2:00 - 2:30 PMAwards Ceremony

Competition

The main competition will be 4 hours long and will consist of a number of programming challenges that teams must work together to solve. For specifics on grading and scoring, read the information on the rules page and check out our sample problems.

The fun round will be half an hour packed with fun programming puzzles, including code golf, esoteric languages, and anything else we can think of!

Registration

Registration is now open! Each competitor needs to fill out the following form: https://tinyurl.com/mbit2019. Sign-ups are provided on a first-come, first-served basis. For information on what division to choose, visit the rules page.

Each team will need an adult sponsor. The sponsor is not required to stay for the whole event, but should be available in the event of an emergency. Multiple teams can have the same sponsor (for example, a teacher may be the sponsor of all teams from a school).

We will also provide lunch, so let us know in the form about any dietary restrictions we may need to accommodate. T-shirts will be available for $10; if you want one, enter your size on the form. If you have any questions, or if something has changed after filling out the registration form, please send us an email at mbit.organizers@gmail.com. We hope to see you there!
